Why this check?
Did you know? The RDW (red blood cell distribution width) is an underestimated ageing marker: it shows variability in blood cell production and is one of the strongest predictors of biological age.
The connection Albumin is the most important transport protein and decreases with age — with poor nutrition, chronic inflammation or impaired liver function. A low albumin level correlates with accelerated ageing.
Often overlooked Biological Age ist keine fixe Zahl — es ist messbar und veränderbar. Menschen, die regelmäßig ihren Aging-Score tracken, können durch gezielte Interventionen messbar jünger werden.
