Why this check?
Did you know? Chronic diseases like type 2 diabetes, heart disease or kidney failure develop for years without symptoms. Annual blood checks can uncover this development early.
The connection The HbA1c value shows the average blood sugar over the last 3 months — making it far more meaningful than a single glucose measurement.
Often overlooked Iron and TSH are often the silent causes of persistent fatigue. Both are included in the Long Term Health Check — and are often not measured by GPs.
