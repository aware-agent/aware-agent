Why this check?
Did you know? Strictly speaking, Vitamin D is not a vitamin but a hormone — it influences over 200 genes in the body, including almost all immune processes, calcium regulation and mood.
The connection A value above 50 ng/ml is considered optimal — most Germans are between 20 and 30 ng/ml. Without a test, you don't know whether your supplementation is sufficient or whether you're underdosing.
Often overlooked Particularly at risk: people who spend a lot of time indoors, have dark skin or live in the north. But many also don't reach the optimal level in summer.
